About Me

Argyris Konstantinidis is a Greek filmmaker based in Athens, Greece. He is currently completing his studies at the University of West Attica, in the Department of Photography and Audiovisual Arts.

Since 2022, he has been actively involved in various creative projects, including short films, documentaries, and social media content. Together with his collaborators, he has produced a short film titled "TABLAS" and a documentary about hip-hop culture in Paris, "Bridging the Gap."

In addition to his personal work, he has collaborated with several creative teams, such as CopWatch.gr, contributing to the documentary "There's a War Going On Outside."

In the field of social media, he has worked with prominent YouTube channels and TikTok pages, including Konilo and foodforhealth.gr.

He is currently working as a freelance filmmaker, with HotBox Music Studio as one of his main partners.
